Author: Taufik Nurrohman

> Enable Mecha text editor in your public comment form.

You don&rsquo;t need any external resources at all. This plugin will only take your `editor.min.js` and `mte.min.js` file from your `manager\assets\sword\editor` folder to be applied in your public comment form.
